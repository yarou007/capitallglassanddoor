Storefront Glass Repair FAQ

Can you board up first and replace later?

Yes. Board-up is often the safest immediate action while replacement materials are prepared.

Do you handle occupied business locations?

Yes. We work with site contacts to stage repair around active business operations when possible.

Can you pair glass repair with door hardware fixes?

Yes. We can coordinate hardware adjustments or repairs when door function is affected.
