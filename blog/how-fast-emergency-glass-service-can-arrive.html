Emergency Response

How Fast Can Emergency Glass Service Arrive?

Response time depends on route density, traffic conditions, active call volume, and site access constraints. Here is how to speed up dispatch.

What Affects ETA

  • Current route congestion and weather conditions.
  • Distance from active crews to your location.
  • Type of opening and required immediate response.
  • Site access barriers (gates, docks, security protocols).

How to Improve Dispatch Speed

  1. Call directly instead of form-only submission for emergencies.
  2. Share exact address and best arrival entry point.
  3. Send clear photos by text when possible.
  4. Confirm whether the opening is unsecured.
