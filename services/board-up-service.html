When Board-Up Is the Right First Step

  • Glass type requires fabrication time
  • After-hours breakage with immediate risk
  • Weather exposure threatens interior damage
  • Security cannot wait for replacement lead time

What Happens After Secure-Up

  1. Opening is stabilized and protected.
  2. Measurements and scope are documented.
  3. Replacement glass or door work is planned.
  4. Final install is scheduled and completed.
