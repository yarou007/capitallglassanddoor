Insurance and Cost

Is Storefront Glass Repair Covered by Insurance?

Coverage depends on your policy and incident type, but better documentation often improves claim clarity and speed.

Documentation Checklist

  • Photo evidence of damage before temporary secure-up.
  • Time/date of incident and any security reports.
  • Written scope summary from service provider.
  • Separate line items for emergency secure-up vs final replacement.

This guide is informational, not legal or policy advice. Always confirm directly with your carrier. We can provide service notes that help support carrier conversations.
