Emergency Glass Repair vs Scheduled Glass Replacement

Not every incident needs the same response timeline. Use this framework to choose between immediate emergency work and planned replacement.

Call (703) 244-0559Text UsRequest Service

Choose Emergency Response When

  • The opening is unsafe or unsecured.
  • The incident happened after-hours and site risk is active.
  • Operations cannot continue without immediate stabilization.

Choose Scheduled Replacement When

  • The opening is secured and temporary controls are in place.
  • Custom glass or hardware requires lead time.
  • The issue is non-urgent and can be planned around operations.

Hybrid Workflow Is Common

  • Board-up now, replacement later is often the safest sequence.
  • Immediate cleanup and stabilization reduce further damage.
  • Scheduled return allows better material and install planning.

Questions to Ask Before Deciding

  • Is anyone at risk if we wait?
  • Can the opening be secured today?
  • Do we have enough scope details to schedule efficiently?
