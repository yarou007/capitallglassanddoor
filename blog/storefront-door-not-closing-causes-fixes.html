Storefront Door Not Closing Properly: Causes and Fixes

A door that will not close or latch is both a security issue and a customer-safety risk. Here are the most common causes and next steps.

Call (703) 244-0559Text UsRequest Service

Common Causes

  • Door closer failure or incorrect sweep/latch speed.
  • Strike and latch misalignment.
  • Threshold interference or dragging door bottom.
  • Worn pivots, hinges, or panic hardware.

Immediate Temporary Steps

  • Limit access through the affected entry if possible.
  • Do not force the door repeatedly; it can worsen damage.
  • Call for door repair if locking or egress is compromised.

When It Is an Emergency

  • Door cannot lock at close of business.
  • Door cannot close in high-traffic public space.
  • Panic hardware is nonfunctional on required exit path.

What a Repair Visit Should Cover

  • Closer and alignment diagnosis.
  • Hardware adjustment or replacement plan.
  • Verification of reliable latch and safe operation.
