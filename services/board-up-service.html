Emergency Board-Up Service

24/7 Board-Up Service for Unsecured Openings

When replacement glass cannot be installed immediately, board-up is the fastest way to restore security and weather protection. We provide emergency board-up service across Arlington, Washington DC, Baltimore, and the broader DMV routes.

When Board-Up Is the Right First Step

  • Opening is exposed and unsafe after-hours
  • Glass type requires fabrication lead time
  • Weather exposure or security risk is immediate
  • Police or insurance documentation is still in progress

What Happens Next

  1. Emergency secure-up and site stabilization.
  2. Measurement and scope verification.
  3. Replacement material planning and scheduling.
  4. Final repair and operation checks.

Board-Up Service FAQ

Can you provide board-up and glass replacement as one workflow?

Yes. We board up first if needed, then complete replacement once material and site conditions are confirmed.

Do you service storefronts and residential openings?

Yes. We handle both commercial and residential board-up requests.

Do I need to call even if I submitted a form?

Yes for emergencies. Calling is the fastest path to dispatch.
