How to Secure a Business After a Break-In

After a break-in, the goal is to restore safety, security, and control quickly while preserving documentation and minimizing downtime.

Call (703) 244-0559Text UsRequest Service

Immediate Priorities

  • Confirm no one is in danger before entering damaged areas.
  • Contact authorities if required by your incident protocol.
  • Isolate broken glass and compromised entry points.

Document Everything

  • Take wide and close photos before major cleanup.
  • Record damaged openings, locks, and hardware.
  • Capture timestamps and witness notes for reports.

Secure the Site

  • Request emergency board-up for exposed openings.
  • Repair or rekey compromised lock systems.
  • Evaluate adjacent doors and panels for hidden damage.

Stabilize Operations

  • Coordinate with staff on temporary entry plans.
  • Schedule glass and door replacement as soon as scope is confirmed.
  • Notify stakeholders once secure-up is complete.
