Common Commercial Door Hardware Problems

Commercial door hardware issues usually start small and then become urgent. Spotting early warning signs can prevent outages and safety risk.

Call (703) 244-0559Text UsRequest Service

Frequent Failure Points

  • Panic bars that stick or fail to latch.
  • Closers that leak and lose control speed.
  • Misaligned strikes causing failed lock engagement.
  • Worn pivots and hinges causing drag and sag.

Operational Warning Signs

  • Door slams or drifts without control.
  • Entry requires excessive force to open or close.
  • Lock turns but does not secure the opening.

When to Call Immediately

  • Exit device is unreliable on required egress path.
  • Door cannot close or lock after business hours.
  • Hardware damage creates a safety concern for occupants.

Preventive Maintenance Tips

  • Schedule periodic hardware checks before peak seasons.
  • Track repeat complaints by specific door location.
  • Address minor alignment issues before they cascade.
