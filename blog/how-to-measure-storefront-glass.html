Storefront Safety

How to Measure Storefront Glass Without Costly Reorders

Bad measurements delay replacement and increase downtime. Use this field-ready checklist before requesting fabrication.

Measurement Basics

  • Measure visible opening width and height at multiple points.
  • Capture glass thickness and note tempered/laminated markings if visible.
  • Document frame condition, gasket type, and damage points.
  • Take straight-on and angled photos for installer review.

When to Stop and Call a Pro

If glass remains unstable, avoid close measurement and request emergency secure-up. Our team can handle safe measurement during the service visit.
