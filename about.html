Team and Process

How We Work on Every Job

Dispatch and Access Planning

Before arrival we gather opening details, urgency, and access constraints. For dense areas like Downtown DC and Inner Harbor Baltimore, we plan around loading zones, security desk check-ins, and building elevator rules.

Scope Verification

We verify glass type, hardware fit, and safety considerations so work is done correctly the first time. If a temporary board-up is the safest first move, we document next steps and replacement timing.

Execution and Cleanup

Our technicians secure the opening, install or repair components, and test door operation. Work areas are cleaned and documented for handoff.

Follow-Up Support

When parts or custom glass are required, we schedule follow-up with status communication to reduce operational disruptions.
