Board-Up vs Replacement

Board-Up vs Glass Replacement: Which One First?

Many property owners ask whether they should board up or replace immediately. The right answer depends on safety risk, material availability, and timing.

Choose Board-Up First When

  • The opening is exposed and unsafe right now.
  • The glass specification needs custom fabrication.
  • It is after-hours and full replacement cannot be completed safely.

Choose Immediate Replacement When

  • Correct material is available and site conditions allow safe install.
  • Access is clear and work can be completed without additional risk.
  • Replacement restores business operation quickly with no secure-up gap.

Most urgent incidents begin with board-up followed by scheduled replacement through storefront glass repair.
