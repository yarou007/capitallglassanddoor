Tempered vs Laminated Storefront Glass: Practical Comparison

Glass selection depends on opening type, security needs, and building requirements. This article gives a practical overview for non-technical stakeholders.

Tempered Glass Overview

Tempered glass is widely used for safety glazing in many storefront and door applications due to its break pattern characteristics.

Laminated Glass Overview

Laminated glass can provide additional retention benefits because interlayers help hold fragments in place after breakage.

How to Decide

  • Review opening location and risk profile.
  • Confirm project requirements with a qualified installer.
  • Align selection with safety and code expectations.
