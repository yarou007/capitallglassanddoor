Emergency Guide

What to Expect From a 24/7 Glass Repair Call

Knowing what dispatch needs up front helps your emergency move faster. Here is the typical call flow and what to have ready.

Call (703) 244-0559Text UsRequest Service

What Dispatch Will Ask

  • Exact location and best technician access point.
  • Type of opening and what failed (storefront, door glass, window).
  • Whether the opening is currently unsecured.

What to Prepare Before Arrival

  • Photos of damage and surrounding conditions.
  • On-site contact name and phone.
  • Building access info (loading, security, gate codes if applicable).

What Happens On-Site

  • Safety-first assessment and immediate secure-up actions.
  • Cleanup and stabilization of the affected opening.
  • Board-up or replacement workflow based on scope and materials.

After the Emergency

  • You receive next-step guidance and timing.
  • Follow-up replacement is scheduled when needed.
  • Additional door or lock issues can be handled in the same workflow.
