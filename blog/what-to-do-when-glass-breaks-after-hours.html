Emergency Response

What to Do When Glass Breaks After Hours

If glass breaks late at night, your first actions should focus on safety and securing the opening. This guide explains a practical response sequence.

Step-by-Step Emergency Checklist

  1. Move people away from the area and prevent foot traffic near shards.
  2. If safe, isolate the space and avoid touching unstable glass edges.
  3. Call a 24/7 emergency glass team and provide location plus opening type.
  4. If replacement cannot happen immediately, request board-up service.
  5. Document the incident with photos for internal records or insurance communication.

What Dispatch Needs From You

  • Address and best entry instructions.
  • Whether the opening is unsecured.
  • Storefront, door lite, or window type.
  • Any building-manager access protocols.
