Emergency Door Repair for Secure, Safe Entry

When doors do not latch, drag, slam, or fail to secure, operations stall and safety risks rise. We repair emergency door issues in Arlington, Washington DC, Baltimore, and nearby markets for offices, storefronts, residential entries, and managed properties.

  • Dragging door bottoms and threshold interference

Emergency Door Repair FAQ

Can you repair commercial door closers and panic bars same day?

Many closer and panic hardware issues are corrected same visit depending on opening condition and parts requirements.

Do you service aluminum storefront doors?

Yes. We repair common storefront door issues including latching, alignment, closer function, and lock interface problems.

Do you also replace damaged door glass when needed?

Yes. We coordinate door repair with glass replacement when both components are part of the failure.
