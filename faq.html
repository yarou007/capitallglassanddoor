Frequently Asked Questions

Emergency Glass, Door, and Locksmith FAQ

For active emergencies, calling is always fastest. The FAQs below help you decide next steps and know what to expect.

How quickly can you respond to emergency glass repair in Arlington, DC, or Baltimore?

Response depends on traffic, weather, and current dispatch load. We prioritize unsecured openings and provide live ETA updates during dispatch.

Do you provide emergency board-up if glass cannot be replaced immediately?

Yes. We secure the opening first and schedule measured replacement to restore full operation as quickly as possible.

Can you repair commercial door closers, panic bars, and hardware the same day?

Many repairs are completed on the first visit depending on parts availability and opening condition. We also provide follow-up planning when full hardware replacement is needed.

What types of storefront glass do you service?

We support tempered and laminated storefront panels, door lites, sidelites, and transom glass in retail and commercial buildings.

Do you handle residential window and patio door glass?

Yes. We provide residential glass repair and replacement for cracked windows, patio door glass, and related openings.

Do you offer locksmith services too?

Yes. We provide lockout support, rekeys, lock changes, and lock hardware service for commercial and residential properties.

What details should I provide on the call?

Share your location, type of opening affected, urgency, photos if available, and any site access limits (gate code, loading dock, building manager contact).

Are your installations safety-compliant?

We review opening requirements and apply safety glazing guidance including ANSI Z97.1 and CPSC 16 CFR 1201 considerations where applicable.
